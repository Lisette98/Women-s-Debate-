Bernie Sanders

The 2016 presidential campaign of Bernie Sanders, the junior United States Senator and former Congressman from Vermont, began with a formal announcement by Sanders on May 26, 2015. Sanders says that his campaign will focus on the income and wealth inequality that he claims has eroded the American middle class, and campaign finance reform.
