Women's Rights are Human Rights

Shouldn't the candidates for president be debating issues and policies that impact us all? For example, raising women’s workforce participation to the same level as men’s could raise per capital GDP by at least 5%.

Making Informed Decisions

Women make up more that 50% of the US population and are a vital voice in moving our country forward. Did you know that America is the only developed nation that does not guarantee paid family and medical leave?

Transparency and Accountability

Presidential candidates have become stuck on one issue as it relates to women- abortion. The Women’s Debate helps focus their attention on other issues that will determine our future.
